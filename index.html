AKAN NAMING SYSYTEM

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.Children are given their first names as a 'day name'. This name corresponds to the day of the week in which they were born.

MALE

  1. Sunday: Kwasi
  2. Monday: Kwadwo
  3. Tuesday: Kwabena
  4. Wednesday: Kwaku
  5. Thursday: Yaw
  6. Friday: Kofi
  7. Saturday: Kwame

FEMALE

  1. Sunday: Akosua
  2. Monday: Adwoa
  3. Tuesday: Abenaa
  4. Wednesday: Akua
  5. Thursday: Yaa
  6. Friday: Afua
  7. Saturday: Ama
