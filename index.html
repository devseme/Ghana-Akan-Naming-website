AKAN NAMING SYSYTEM

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Children are given their first names as a 'day name'. This name corresponds to the day of the week in which they were born. This websites helps you generate your Akhan name. All you have to do is to input your date of birth, select your gender, then submit to know your Akan name.
